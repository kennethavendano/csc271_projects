What is Fantasy Football?

Fantasy football is a game where you act as the manager of a virtual team composed of real-life NFL players, competing against other managers in a league. Your team's success depends on the actual, statistical performance of those players in real-world games, which generate points based on various actions like touchdowns, yards, and catches. You draft your players, set weekly lineups, and can make trades to build a championship-caliber team and win weekly head-to-head matchups within your league.

How To Play:

  1. Draft a Team
  2. Set Your Lineup
  3. Earn Points
  4. Compete Weekly
  5. Make Trades
  6. Win the Playoffs
